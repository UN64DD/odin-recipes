Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and delicious meal made from cooked rice, vegetables, eggs, and your choice of protein.

It's an excellent way to use leftover rice.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Green onions
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Cook garlic and vegetables.
  3. Add eggs and scramble.
  4. Add cooked rice.
  5. Pour in soy sauce.
  6. Mix well and serve.
