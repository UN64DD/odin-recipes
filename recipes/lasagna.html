Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

It is perfect for family dinners and can be prepared ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmessa cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauces.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
    6. Let it cool before serving.
