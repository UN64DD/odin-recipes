Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats served with butter, syrup, fruits, or whipped cream.

They are simple to make and loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot pan.
  5. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup.
